About Me

Hi, I'm Nainoa Shizuru - a designer currently living in SLC, Utah + working at Rally Interactive. I aim to empower people and simplify their everyday lives by creating beautiful, usable experiences. Prior to getting into any design tool, I like to first think beyond the pixels. Seeking out the problem at hand and understanding the overall story so I'm able to focus on the right aesthetic for the right problem.

Outside of designing, I like to surf and hike (pretty much any outdoor activity). I'm also passionate about lifestyle and landscape photography because it's an incredible medium that can inspire, connect people to the subject, and convey emotions. Other than that, I'm currently taking on freelance projects that are both interesting and challenging. So hit me up if you want to chat about a project or just say hi.
